About Virtue Careers

Prosper Olaoluwa
Founder & Executive Career Positioning Strategist

Virtue Careers exists because too many exceptional professionals are overlooked — not for lack of experience, but because that experience is never translated into a language decision-makers immediately recognize. Directors, VPs, and executives are passed over for promotions and leadership roles not because they lack results, but because those results — the achievements, the metrics, the measurable impact — are buried instead of highlighted.

Founded by an executive career positioning strategist with years of experience helping senior professionals present themselves as the obvious choice for leadership opportunities, Virtue Careers is built on one principle: experience creates capability, but positioning creates opportunity.

We don't write generic resumes or apply one-size-fits-all templates. Every resume, LinkedIn profile, and career document we create is built to show boards, hiring managers, and decision-makers exactly why you belong in the room — and why you're the right choice once you're in it.
