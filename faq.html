Frequently Asked Questions

How is this different from using a template or ChatGPT?

Templates and AI tools can format words on a page, but they don't understand how recruiters, boards, and hiring executives actually evaluate candidates. Every document we create is strategically positioned — built around your specific achievements, target role, and industry expectations, not a generic structure.

Will my documents be ATS-compliant?

Yes. Every resume and career document is built to pass Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS) and benchmarked against recruitment parsing standards used across major platforms — while still reading naturally and compellingly to a human hiring manager once it reaches them.

I'm not actively job searching right now — is this still for me?

Yes. Many clients come to us before they need to move — to be ready when the right opportunity, promotion, or leadership transition appears. Strong positioning is most powerful when it's already in place, not built in a rush after an opportunity has already passed.

Do you work with career changers, industry switchers, or people seeking promotions — not just job seekers?

Yes. Whether you're pursuing a promotion within your current company, transitioning into a new industry, or repositioning your experience for a different function, we tailor your narrative to align with where you're going — not just where you've been.

Do you only work with clients in the U.S.?

No. While much of our work focuses on the U.S. market, we work with professionals and executives across North America, South America, Europe, Asia, and Africa — helping global talent position themselves for opportunities anywhere in the world.

How much does it cost?

Pricing depends on the scope of support you need. Standalone services like a resume refresh or LinkedIn optimization start at an accessible entry price, while comprehensive executive packages are custom-quoted based on your goals. See our Packages page, or book a discovery call for exact pricing.

How long does the process take?

Most projects are completed within 5–10 business days, depending on scope and package. Rush timelines can be accommodated when needed — this can be discussed during your discovery call.
